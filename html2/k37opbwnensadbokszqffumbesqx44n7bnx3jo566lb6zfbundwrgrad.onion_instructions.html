Order Instructions

All the details how to make an order with us
Product Selection

Select the product you want on the Products & Prices page and click on the "Order Now" button.

Select Shipping Option

Choose one of the shipping options: Free, Express or OverNight and fill in the relevant form then click the "Pay with Bitcoin" button. (If you choose money transfer via paypal or western union, you will fill in your email address which for sending order details, then click "Pay with Bitcoin" button)

Bitcoin Paying

Follow the instructions on the Bitcoin payment page.

Fill in an order form

After making the payment, you will be taken to the necessary details page for your order. Please make sure that you fill in the shipping or transfer information correctly. If you make a mistake in one of them, you can contact us by email and provide us with the correct information.

Tracking Details Shipping / Money Transfer

If you selected a credit card and express or overnight delivery method you will receive a tracking number for your shipment or if you selected money transfer by Western Union you will receive an MTCN code for tracking and receiving money.

Receiving the product

In the choice of money transfers, the money is sent via Paypal up to two hours and through Western Union for up to 7 hours. In the choice of credit card, the shipment will depend on the choice of shipping method: free shipping will take between 5-8 days, express shipping will take 3-5 days and overnight shipping will take between one and two days.
