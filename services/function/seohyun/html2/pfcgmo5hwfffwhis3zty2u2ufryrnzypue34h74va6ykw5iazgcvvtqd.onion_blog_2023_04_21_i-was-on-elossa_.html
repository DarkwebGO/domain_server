I was on Elossa

Elossa is a podcast run by Elokapina, the Finnish section of Extinction Rebellion. We talk about planning, its history and especially how planning relates to the climate struggle. We also talk about the Swedish government's inability to deal with the situation.

Regarding XR, I am skeptical of their methods, mostly due to the focus on tactics to the detriment of strategy. But hey, at least they're doing something.

The audio is somewhat bad, I think due to transcoding to too low bitrate on Jesse's end.
