Verify a Facebook email

You don't know what is the email address associated to a facebook's profile? Do you have an email address but you are not sure if it is the correct or no? Or maybe you don't have idea about the email address but you want to get a clue about it.
Don't matter what is your case, below we will give you some instructions to get the email address from a Facebook account.

Step Nº1 - Get the account username

All the facebook profiles have an username. To get the username the only that you must do is access to the profile.
Once you accessed, you will see the profile URL in your browser. Something like this:

In the example obove, the URL is: https://www.facebook.com/charles.black55, so the profile's username is: charles.black55
Sometimes, the URL can be longer, because it can include some others parameters. If you see the symbol ?, this will be the end of the username. For example, if you see the following URL: https://www.facebook.com/charles.black55?fref=ts, you must skip everything after symbol ? (inslusive).

Step Nº2 - Go to the recover page

Once you get the username, if you are logged inside of your own facebook account, you must log out.
Once you logged our from your account, do click here and you will see something like this:

Now, you must write the username in the form and click on SEARCH.

Step Nº3 - Analyze the answer

After complete the step before, you will see something like this:

What we have done is initiate a password retrieve process. But don't worry, becase we will not complete the process and the real owner of the account will never note anything.
Facebook will offer us different ways to retrieve the password, and one of these ways will be send a message to the account's associated email address, and they will give you a clue about the email address. You will not see the entire email address, but with the clue will be enough to verify if the email that you have is the associated or not.

Suppose you believe that the associated email address is onlineservice@dwmail.top. After following these steps you will confirm that YES, the email address that you have is the correct.

And what if you don't have an email address? Unfortunately there is no way to find out what is the email address. This is the best that you can do. Doing this steps, you can get a some clue. For example, in this example, you know that the email address is @hotmail.com and you also know that start with a c, and end with a 3 and there are 9 characters between the first and last characters.
Generally, discover an email address is easy, because people use their own names, and birth dates, etc.
At other times, it will be much more difficult to discover.

Step Nº4 - Cancel everything done

Once you ended with all, you must click on "Not you?" and you will back to the step Nº2. Now, all is ended and you can log in again in your account.

Extra - Check by email

If you don't have any idea about the email address and you want to get it, once you get the clue, you can make different combinations and test if it is the correct or not. In the same way that you searched for the email address using the facebook's username, you can do it using an email address. If the entered email address doesn't exists, facebook will inform you.
