Ukraine, The Road to War (Introduction)

It's been already 5 months since the start of Russia's so-called Special Military Operation. From the very beginning of this military intervention (February 24, 2022) into the Ukrainian territory the world has become radically polarized under the direct influence of media outlets. It literally seemed that just by the next day the West rallied under the US umbrella against President Putin's Russia in a way the world has never seen since the October days of the 1962 Cuban missile crisis, when the two archenemies, The United States of America and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, where on the brink to initiate a direct confrontation leading to a nuclear war.

It is my intention to start this blog analyzing key figures and facts that have lead us all into this new scenario that should worry us. If you are not, you should definitely be concerned.
